About Our Department

We are a collective of thinkers, creators, and leaders dedicated to pushing the boundaries of knowledge and technology.

Our department fosters a unique environment where students are encouraged to collaborate across disciplines. From coding the next groundbreaking app to analyzing market trends, orchestrating cultural festivals, and managing large-scale events, our students are at the forefront of innovation. We believe in learning by doing, and our vibrant student societies are the heart of this philosophy.

What we do
Tech Society

Welcome to the bleeding edge. Our society is for anyone passionate about technology, from software development and AI to cybersecurity and robotics. We host weekly coding challenges, workshops on the latest frameworks, and talks from industry leaders. Your journey to becoming a tech visionary starts here.

Finance Society

The Finance Society empowers students to master financial skills by collecting and managing funds, fostering learning through competitions and seminars, and building networks with industry professionals.

Arts & Culture Society

Unleash your creative spirit! The Arts & Culture society is a vibrant community for painters, poets, musicians, and performers. We organize open mic nights, art exhibitions, theater productions, and cultural festivals that enrich campus life. Join us to collaborate, create, and inspire.

Events Crew

We are the architects of experience. The Events Crew is the dynamic team behind the department's most exciting happenings, from formal galas and academic conferences to concerts and campus-wide competitions. Gain hands-on experience in logistics, marketing, and team management.
